About the Tool

In the face of accelerating climate change, resilience and adaptation are no longer optional strategies—they are essential. As our global ecosystems experience rising temperatures, more frequent extreme weather events, and shifting climate patterns, communities, governments, and businesses must take proactive steps to address these changes. One of the most powerful ways to facilitate this is through the use of Climate Change Resilience and Adaptation Tools (CCRATs). These tools enable us to understand, plan for, and respond to the impacts of climate change in a way that minimizes risk and maximizes long-term sustainability.
